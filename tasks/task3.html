Steganography

What is steganography?

Steganography is the practice of concealing information within another message or physical object to avoid detection.

Some examples of steganography involve embedding a secret piece of text inside a picture, video or audio file.

You could also hide a secret message or script inside of a Word or Excel document.

How we will use it today.

Recently we have found evidence of multiple different images being used to relay hidden messages between a well-known criminals. You have been hired by the local police force to decrypt a message by using steganography on the images found to find out the name of the suspect and what the hidden passkey they use to encrypt their hidden message.
